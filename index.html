What Is Menstruation?

Menstruation, often called a period, is normal monthly shedding of the lining of the uterus(womb) in women.This happens because the uterus prepares for a potential pregnancy each month,and if pregnancy doesn't occur, it sheds the lining.The shed lining, along with blood, comes out through the vagina.
